About Us

Our Story

Community Connector is a non-profit founded by two students at Rise Academy. We are eleventh grade students at Rise Academy, and our mission is to make a impact in today's society, and help those in need. Our organization operates in the Bay Area, primarily in the Tri-Valley and Fremont Area.

Why?

There are many senior citizens and people who just don't have the funds and are desperately in need of help. The Covid 19 pandemic has left many of them stuck inside their homes, and with no one to help them. It is everyone's duty to help people in need, and they're the people who need it the most right now.

We are looking for people to go and get essentials and groceries for those who are in need. That includes the elderly as well as anyone who does not have the money, due to the current situation.

With your help, we can expand our community and build a better future!
